<objective>
Prove the runtime behavior for Phase 04's channel and AI surfaces, with special attention to the M04 WhatsApp smoke path and the M05 assistive AI behaviors that must never auto-send.
</objective>

<threat_model>
## Threat Model

- Sensitive channel credentials and service-role Supabase access must stay server-side.
- WhatsApp webhook handling must not expose Evolution credentials to the browser.
- Inbound processing must remain idempotent and must not duplicate messages or tags on retries.
- AI outputs must remain assistive only; no autonomous message sending is allowed.
- Triage, sentiment, and next-action recommendations must fail closed without blocking conversation creation or delivery.
- External smoke validation depends on real device + production credentials; missing prerequisites must be recorded as blocker evidence rather than hidden.
</threat_model>
